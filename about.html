About Me

I’m Xavier, a 21-year-old from Queensbridge, NY. This space is where I document growth in real time. From content creation and video editing to photography and building technical skills, this site reflects what I’m learning and who I’m becoming. I create videos and write about games and manga that stay with me — exploring symbolism, character arcs, and the ideas. Some work is experimental. Some is polished. Over time, this will evolve into both a creative archive and a professional portfolio — a record of the skills I build and the ideas I care enough to explore.
