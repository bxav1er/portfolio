# Interaction Design - xavierbzz Personal Website

## Core Philosophy
A minimal, intentional digital space that serves as an archive for work, thoughts, and creative explorations. The design prioritizes readability, longevity, and calm presence over promotional content.

## Interaction Components

### 1. Project Timeline Browser
- **Function**: Interactive timeline of projects and experiments
- **Interaction**: Horizontal scrollable timeline with project cards
- **Content**: Each card shows project title, date, status (in-progress/completed), and brief description
- **Details**: Click to expand with more information, links, and related content
- **Visual**: Minimal cards with subtle hover states, no flashy animations

### 2. Writing & Thoughts Filter
- **Function**: Categorized browsing of writings and notes
- **Interaction**: Filter buttons for categories (Tech, Manga, Games, Personal Growth, Photography)
- **Content**: Blog posts, short thoughts, analysis pieces, learning notes
- **Details**: Grid layout with titles, dates, and excerpt preview
- **Visual**: Clean typography-focused design with reading time estimates

### 3. Photography Gallery
- **Function**: Simple, fast-loading photo gallery
- **Interaction**: Grid layout with lightbox view for larger images
- **Content**: Personal photography, visual experiments, moments captured
- **Details**: Minimal metadata (date, location if relevant)
- **Visual**: Consistent aspect ratios, subtle hover effects

### 4. Contact & Connection Hub
- **Function**: Simple contact form and social links
- **Interaction**: Clean form with name, email, message fields
- **Content**: Contact options, availability status, social platforms
- **Details**: Personal email options, content platform links
- **Visual**: Minimal form design with clear feedback states

## Navigation Structure
- **Home**: Personal introduction and current focus
- **Projects**: Timeline of work and experiments  
- **Writing**: Thoughts, analysis, and learning notes
- **Visual**: Photography and visual work
- **About/Contact**: Personal information and connection options

## User Experience Flow
1. **Discovery**: Visitors land on home page with clear introduction
2. **Exploration**: Navigate through projects, writing, or visual work
3. **Engagement**: Read content, view projects, browse photography
4. **Connection**: Contact form or social platform links for further engagement

## Technical Considerations
- Fast loading times with minimal JavaScript
- Responsive design for all devices
- Accessible navigation and content structure
- SEO-friendly content organization
- Long-term maintainability and content management
